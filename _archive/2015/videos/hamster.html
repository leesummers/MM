Hamster on a Wheel

By re-contextualizing what is a common occurrence in the life of a hamster I hope to highlight the absurdity of our attempts to commune with animals. Hamsters in particular are often a child's first pet and therefore represent a defining moment in the development of human-animal relationships. It is this type of learning experience that ultimately leads to an attitude of superiority towards non-human animals that I am calling into question with this video.
