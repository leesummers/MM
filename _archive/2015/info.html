Info

Matthew Moore is an Assistant Professor and Coordinator of the Photography Program at Anne Arundel Community College in Arnold, Maryland. Matthew received a BFA degree in photography from the College for Creative Studies in Detroit, Michigan in 2000. He earned his MFA degree in 2009 from Georgia State University in Atlanta, Georgia. While studying at Georgia State, Matthew was awarded the Chandler Award in 2006 and Ernest G. Welch Graduate Photography Award in 2007.

In 2014 Matthew was an artist-in-residence at the Vilnius Academy of Arts' Nida Art Colony, in Neringa, Lithuania. He is currently an artist-in-residence at the Maryland Hall for the Creative Arts in Annapolis, Maryland.

In addition to teaching, Matthew also contributes to a number of regional and national magazines. His editorial clients include Hour Detroit Magazine, Detroit Home, XXL, Mass Appeal, and Cancer Today. In 2004, his documentary, "A Tale of Two Cities," won a silver medal for Best Photo Essay in a City or Regional Magazine.
