Info

Matthew Moore is an Assistant Professor and Coordinator of the Photography Program at Anne Arundel Community College in Arnold, Maryland. Matthew received his MFA degree in 2009 from Georgia State University in Atlanta, Georgia. While there he was awarded the Ernest G. Welch Graduate Photography Award in 2007 and the Chandler Award in 2006. He received a BFA degree in 2000 from the College for Creative Studies in Detroit, Michigan. After completing his BFA, Matthew moved to the Czech Republic and has continued to return there frequently to exhibit work and lecture, most recently at Univerzita J. E. Purkyne (2009) and Prague College (2010). Matthew has also been a long time contributor to Hour Detroit Magazine and his 2004 documentary, "A Tale of Two Cities," won a silver medal for Best Photo Essay in a City or Regional Magazine. Other editorial clients include Detroit Home, XXL, Mass Appeal, and Cancer Today.
