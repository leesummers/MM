Matthew Moore received a BFA in Photography from the College for Creative Studies in Detroit, Michigan in 2000 and an MFA in Photography from Georgia State University in Atlanta, Georgia in 2009. Matthew's work examines the contemporary landscape, with a focus on the way historical narratives are controlled through human intervention. Matthew is the recipient of Maryland State Arts Council Individual Artist Awards in 2015 and 2018. Recent residencies include the Vilnius Academy of Arts' Nida Art Colony in Nida, Lithuania and the Joshua Tree Highlands AIR program in Joshua Tree, CA. Matthew is currently an Associate Professor of Photography at Anne Arundel Community College in Arnold, Maryland.
